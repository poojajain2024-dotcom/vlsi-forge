## Full description
VLSI Forge helps electronics and VLSI students prepare for college exams and job
interviews in one place.

Features:
- 1000+ MCQs across Digital Electronics, Verilog, STA, SystemVerilog, UVM, SVA,
  Physical Design, and Bus Protocols — each with explanations.
- GeeksforGeeks-style tutorials for fast concept revision.
- Coding practice with test cases and timed scoring.
- Instant quiz scoring with grade, accuracy, and time taken.
- Interview question packs tagged by topic.
- Rate the app and send improvement feedback.

Free: practice MCQs, tutorials, notes, and core coding.
Premium (INR 299/month): full interview question sets across all subjects.

Privacy Policy

App / Service: VLSI Forge

Last updated: 2026

Overview

VLSI Forge is an education app and website for VLSI exam and interview preparation. This policy explains what information we handle and how.

Information we collect

  • Account info (optional): if you sign up, your email and a securely hashed password.
  • Learning activity: quiz scores, coding submissions, and feedback you submit, used to show your progress.
  • Feedback: ratings and comments you choose to send. A display name is optional.

How we use information

  • To provide learning content, track your scores, and improve the app.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

Data storage

Data is stored on our hosted backend database. Passwords are never stored in plain text.

Children

The app is intended for students and professionals and is not directed at children under 13.

Your choices

You can use most content without an account. To request deletion of your account data, contact us using the email below.
